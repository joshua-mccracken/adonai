פעם

following the meaning of פ ע ם (to strike, to beat) 

an anvil
a step/footstep (a tread of the foot)
this time/now/once (הפעם)

exodus 9 27
פרעה sends for משה and אהרן and says to them that this time he has sinned. פרעה calls יהוה righteous and him and his people wicked

exodus 10 17
פרעה pleaded with משה and אהרן to bear (forgive) his sin just this one time. he then asked that they plead with יהוה to take away this death from him
