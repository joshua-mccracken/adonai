tsafah צ-פ-ה

to shine or to be bright, thus:

to enlighten with the eyes, thus:

to look out, pa'al
to view, pa'al

used of prophets, who like watchmen, declare future events by looking out into divinely-given visions, thus:

to forsee, pi'el
to expect, pi'el
to look forward to, pi'el

to lay out, pa'al
to lay over, pa'al

as in the original meaning of to shine or to make bright to make splendid, thus:

to overlay with gold or silver, pi'el
to overlay/coat/plait with metal, pi'el
